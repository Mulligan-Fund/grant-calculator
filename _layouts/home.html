Find the True Value of your Grant Application

The Net Grant Calculator helps both not-for-profits and grantmakers evaluate the net value a grant will provide. For not-for-profits, the calculator provides a tool to help decide which grants to pursue.

For grantmakers, the calculator helps understand the impact on grantees of application and management processes and helps identify ways to increase their grants' net value to grantees.
